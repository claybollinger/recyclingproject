Appliances & Electronics

Appliance Information

Residents who have city waste collection service can request a pickup of appliances by calling LexCall at 311 or (859) 425-2255.

There is no charge for picking up these appliances. Appliances containing Freon will be accepted, but appliances containing oil are not. Pickups are normally made within five business days of your request. This service is for households only.

All Fayette County residents and apartment complexes can recycle appliances at no cost by dropping them off to Environmental Recycling Inc.

3899 Winchester Road
Monday-Friday
7 a.m.-3 p.m.

Other opportunities for appliance recycling or repurposing include Kentucky Utilities Refrigerator and Freezer Recycling program and Habitat for Humanity's ReStore. If you are interested in these programs, please contact the organizations directly.

Acceptable Appliacances

The city offers curb-side collection for the following:

  • Air Conditioners

  • Freezers

  • Refrigerators

  • Water Heaters

  • Washers

  • Dryers

  • Stoves

  • Furnaces

Unacceptable Appliances

The City does not offer curb-side collection for:

  • humidifiers

  • TV's

  • microwaves

  • computers

  • printers

Please take these items to the Electronic Recycling Center.

ELECTRONICS INFORMATION

The city Electronics Recycling Center accepts residential electronic waste Monday through Saturday. The Center does not accept items from businesses, including home businesses, or nonprofit organizations such as churches and schools. This service is for Fayette County residents only.

Electronic Recycling Center
1306 Versailles Road
Lexington, KY 40508

Acceptable Electronics

  • Computers, printers, copiers

  • Printer cartridges and toner cases

  • Cell phones and other communication devices

  • Microwave ovens

  • Televisions and monitors

  • CDs, DVDs and their cases

  • VHS and cassette tapes

  • Audio and video players/equipment

  • Small corded electrical items such as toasters, hair dryers, or radios

  • Extension cords

  • Holiday lights

  • Fluorescent light tubes

  • MP3 players and handheld games

  • Laptops and electronic tablets

  • Rechargeable batteries (We do not accept automotive or other lead-acid batteries. Batteries Plus will accept all batteries for recycling at a cost of $0.50 per pound.)




Unacceptable Electronics

  • Compact fluorescent lightbulbs (Home Depot and Lowe's will take them for free.)

  • Items that are not structurally intact, such as TVs with broken screens. (These items should be taken to the Bluegrass Regional Transfer Station at 1505 Old Frankfort Pike.)

  • Automotive and other lead-acid batteries (Batteries Plus will accept all batteries for recycling at a cost of $0.50 per pound.)
